brisbane, australia

nicholas brown was born in Perth, Western Australia in 1997 and relocated to Brisbane in early 2011. During his time living in Brisbane he attended classes of Film & Television, Graphic Design, Business and more.

At the age of 17 he took the knowledge he had learnt studying the various art forms and launched his own Video & Graphics Production company which has continued to grow. Nic was approached by a local Brisbane photographer who was looking to take their business in its next direction. Video.

He has worked along side Mel of Meloo Studios for many years, they have gone on many adventures domestically and internationally to build such a diverse and entertaining portfolio for both sides of their cliental.
